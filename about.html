About Emmanuel

Emmanuel is an author, preacher, songwriter, and poet. He also writes other creative and inspirational works like essays, letters, and proverbs.

Through the word of prophesy, He was separated by God from his mother's womb to take the gospel of hope to the world.

His passion is to help people discover and fulfil their God-ordained purposes. He served as a youth pastor in Winners Chapels International Los Angeles. Currently, he serves as a deacon and minister.

He is happily married to his lovely wife, Mmesoma Emmanuel-Chukwudi, his purpose partner and best friend.

"I was inspired to write my first book as a teenager in high school after I lost my forty years old Dad. It was a traumatic and defining moment in my life. So, I wrote a collection of poems (Anthology) which I dedicated to my Dad. I didn't stop writing from then."...Emmanuel I. Chukwudi

Emmanuel is the Convener of LOVE & LEAD GROUP, SINGLES CORNER, and T & Y GROUP(TEENAGERS AND YOUTHS GROUP), all faith-based non-denominational platforms designed to equip and empower kingdom-minded citizens to walk in God's love and lead in excellence.

His life is a testimony to the life-transforming power of prayer and prophesy. He also transmits his messages from his social media handle(THE SECRET PLACE TV).
